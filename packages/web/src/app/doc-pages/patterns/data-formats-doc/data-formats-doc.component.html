In Elvia we follow the Norwegian standard with the order dd.mm.yyyy for date and hh.mm for time.

Tip

Use dd.mm.yyyy and hh.mm also when using en-US as the locale.

  • 2 digits for month and day - 4 digits for year (dd.mm.yyyy)
  • Punctuation between day, month and year (01.01.2021)
  • In continuous text write out the whole month (January 1)
  • Always lowercase, period, and spaces on day and month (1. januar)
  • If the year is omitted, there must be a period after the day and month. (01.01.)

Use an en-dash (–) without spaces on either side to connect days, dates, and times. (Mac: option + hyphen)

Monday–Friday

16–18

Monday-Friday

Large numbers are arranged in groups of three and three digits. Three-digit numbers and less should be written together. Use commas in decimal numbers.

Phone numbers should be divided into groups of two and two digits.

Telephone numbers in the 8xx series must be written in three groups of three + two + three digits.

Telephone numbers of three, four and five digits are written in one group.
