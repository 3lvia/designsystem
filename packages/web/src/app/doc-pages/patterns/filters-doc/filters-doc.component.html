Filters are made by combining input components to either a top or side filter. Typical components for the most usual filter patterns are:

  • Dropdown
  • Checkbox
  • Search
  • Autocomplete
  • Date picker

Top filters are used on top of tables or lists as an easy way to filter quickly.

Side filters are another way to organize filters and are extra helpful in the scenarios of large sets of data or filters. Filters are collected in a side panel next to the data and can be closed to save space.
