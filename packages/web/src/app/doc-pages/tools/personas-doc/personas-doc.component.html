A persona is a fictional representation of a target audience, embodying the needs and goals of real users—both potential and existing. It may include behaviors, traits, habits, skills, and interests, helping businesses understand their users better.

  1. Do your research. Begin with collecting data about your user group. Make sure to have a plan for how you’re intending on using your persona and let that decide which information you need.
  2. Analyze and summarize. When you’re done collecting data, take some time to analyze the outcome. Which are key elements to summarize a typical user?
  3. Create. Document your persona by using one of the templates, or create your own personalized design that suits your needs. Let the finished persona be a living document that you can update when needed.
