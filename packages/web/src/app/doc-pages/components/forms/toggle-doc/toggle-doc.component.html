You should always have a label next to the toggle to tell the user what they are turning on/off. When stacking multiple toggels vertically, the label should be placed right to align the buttons for ease of use. Place the label on the right or left side of the toggle. The toggle is also available without a given label. You are required to create your custom labels. In case it is required, do not forget to supply the aria-label attribute. The toggles come in two sizes. Regardless of the size you choose, you should never combine the two and always use the same size of toggle throughout the page. When a click event is fired on the toggle, your click handler must change the value of the aria-checked attribute from true to false , or vice versa. The toggle is built up of a checkbox making the keyboard navigation identical to the keyboard navigation for native checkboxes.
