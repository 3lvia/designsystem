There are two different sizes of the date range picker; medium and small. Just like small text fields, the small date range picker is primarily designed for internal systems. Don't mix medium and small size inputs in one layout - choose one or the other and be consistent in your choice throughout the design.

When the screen size becomes smaller than 767px, the date pickers will stack vertically instead of horizontally. When needed, force vertical stacking by setting the isVertical property to true .

Space or Enter to open or close the individual date pickers, as well as to choose different options inside the date picker.
Tab to navigate through the different options inside the date picker, and to navigate between the two date pickers.
