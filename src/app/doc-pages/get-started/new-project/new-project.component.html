How to get started

At the start of a new project we advice that you take a look at the existing templates. This will give you a good insight into how components are used, styled and toned. Feel free to use an existing template as an origin to quickly get started on development. This will contribute a consistent experience for our users. Remember that components can be adapted to your need, but this needs to be clarified with the designer in charge of the Elvis design system.
