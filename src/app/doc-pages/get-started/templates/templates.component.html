Templates At the start of a new project we advice that you take a look at the existing templates. This will give you a good insight in how components are used, style and tone. Feel free to use an existing template as an origin to quickly get started on development. This will contribute a consistent experience for our users. Remember that components can be adapted to your need, but this needs to be clarified with the designer in charge of the Elvis design system. Follow standards for external and internal sites. Create new layouts for existing structures such as articles, factboxes etc.
