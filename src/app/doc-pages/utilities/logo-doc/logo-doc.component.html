Logo

Margin / Padding

The minimum area of padding around the logo is half it's height.

Example: This logo is 100px tall. Which means the padding / margin has to be at least 50px. Making the total element height 200px.

Minimum logo size

The logo should have a height of at least 25px, and then a padding of at least 50% of 25px = 12.25px.

Example (25px height, 12.25px padding)
