Logo Elvia can be described as the power source in your home. Just like our services, our logo acts as the power source that the consumer can turn on and off when needed.

Margin / Padding

The minimum area of padding around the logo is half it's height.

Example: This logo is 100px tall. Which means the spacing has to be at least 50px. Making the total element height 200px.

Minimum logo size

The logo should have a height of at least 25px, and then a spacing of at least 50% of 25px = 12.25px.
